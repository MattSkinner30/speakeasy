Welcome to Speakeasy

Take a tour through our features and activities! Here we will breakdown what we do and how we do it to help you learn your new language.

We have a variety of games to help you learn your new language. Start learning in puzzle and card games.

As you advance through your language, you will start completing crosswords or doing wordsearches.

Quizes at the end of each section will test your knowledge of spelling, words, grammar and characters if your language has a alphabet.

Take Your Learning Anywhere

Learn On Your Desktop

All of the features are browser based and will work in any of the top web browsers.

Or Take It With You

All of the games and activities on Speakeasy have been optimized for our mobile ap, so you don't have to miss anything if you're on the go.
